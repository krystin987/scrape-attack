Bristol Township police are investigating the fatal shooting of an American Bulldog in the Magnolia Hill section of Levittown on Friday morning.

Police were called to Midwood Lane for a report that a neighbor shot and killed the dog after it escaped its yard and attacked the German Shepherd the man was walking.

The unidentified man confirmed to police he fired one shot at the dog. Police did not release any information about any injuries to the German Shepherd. The man had a valid license to carry a firearm, police said.
